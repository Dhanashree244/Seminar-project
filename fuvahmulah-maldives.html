Fuvahmulah, Maldives

Fuvahmulah is a unique tropical island in the Maldives, known for its lush greenery and diverse natural beauty. Unlike many Maldivian islands, Fuvahmulah is a single island atoll with freshwater lakes, rich flora, and abundant wildlife.

This island paradise offers tranquil beaches, vibrant coral reefs, and exceptional opportunities for diving, snorkeling, and exploring nature. Its secluded charm makes it ideal for travelers seeking peace away from the crowds.
